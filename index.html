Our Mission

A new future with trees

The problems our project addresses include:

  • The lack of environmental companies in the market
  • The lack of acknowledgment of population
  • The decrease in tree proportion


Our solution for the problems is creating a platform where a specific AI system will be applied to. This AI system will operate by users taking images of the trees they plant and uploading those images to the system. Once the image is analyzed and confirmed that it is taken in the user's present location, extra bonus points will be added to the userâ€™s account. As the user will plant more trees, he/she will gain more bonus points. Once the bonus points hit a certain limit, digital gifts will be directed towards the users.


We have some unique values our app is established around: Easy accessibility, self-profiting functions, a legal map showing available planting sites will be among those values.


We aim that our early adopters will include:

  • eco-friendly people
  • people who want to make a profit out of the environment
